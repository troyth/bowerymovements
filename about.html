What is this?

This website operates at a different scale of the internet. Instead of the very big, the very flat, and the very banal, here is a place to get super close. Yell at your neighbors, announce whatâ€™s going on the in gallery downstairs, say what the worst, or best, restaurant is. This is an opportunity for the buildings and the people of the Bowery to have a new type of conversation as to what is, and what will be, the Bowery district.

Bowery Movements is a collaboration by: Paul Chan, Dichen Ding, Richard Angus Duff, Jeremy Kim, Jim Stoddart, and Ebberly Strathairn.

This website was created for the A4819 - Web As Site.
Taught by Troy Conrad Therrien and Leigha Dennis.
Columbia University, GSAPP
How does this work?

Each building in the Bowery gets a hashtag of its address (e.g. #135Bowery or #6Delancy ).
